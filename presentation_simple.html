What Does This System Do?

Ask questions in plain English and get instant, accurate answers from all your documents

Example: Customer Support

"What's our refund policy for damaged products?"
Based on your policy documents: Customers can request a full refund for damaged products within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact support with photos of the damage, and we'll process the refund within 5 business days.

Example: HR Department

"How many vacation days do employees get?"
According to the employee handbook: Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 public holidays. Vacation days can be taken after completing 3 months of employment.
